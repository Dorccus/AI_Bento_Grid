An overview of how AI serves us humans:

Everyday Assistance:

Personal Assistants: AI powers virtual assistants like Siri, Alexa, and Google Assistant to help with tasks like setting reminders, answering questions, or controlling smart home devices.

Recommendations: Streaming platforms like Netflix or Spotify use AI to suggest movies, shows, or music tailored to your tastes.

Navigation and Maps: Apps like Google Maps use AI to provide real-time directions, traffic updates, and route optimizations.
